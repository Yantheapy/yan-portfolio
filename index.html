Investment analytics · web application

Board-grade financial decision tool

UAE multi-year medical investment project, as Director of Special Projects, a UAE healthcare & education developer
Challenge

The board needed a defensible go/no-go on a multi-year clinic investment, not a static spreadsheet only one person could read.

What I built

A live parametric model turned into an interactive web app: a six-driver scenario engine (Base / Upside / Downside), live NPV / IRR / MOIC / payback, Monte-Carlo simulation, DCF waterfall, tornado and two-way sensitivity, investor-vs-founder return models, Excel ingestion, EN/RU and PDF export, productizing a multi-thousand-formula model.

Impact

A spreadsheet only one person could read became a board-grade decision tool: any scenario priced in seconds, every assumption visible, the capital call sized on evidence. It informed the board's go/no-go on an AED 240M+ investment programme.

Capital raise · cultural investment

A Dubai cultural institution, made investable

Byzantine & Eastern-Christian heritage museum, Dubai, leading the investment case & capital raise (2025–present)
Challenge

Turn a landmark private collection into an investable institution: a board-ready vehicle a family office or strategic investor could underwrite, not a passion project.

What I built

The full investor package: a multi-year financial model (CAPEX, financing, monthly P&L and cash flow, 5-year IRR / NPV, scenario, sensitivity and Monte-Carlo), a ~70-page business plan, visitor and market analysis benchmarked to Louvre Abu Dhabi and the Met, and the equity and governance structure for the raise.

Positioning

Framed as cultural diplomacy, uniting Byzantine and Middle-Eastern heritage through art, aligned with the UAE’s cultural-tourism strategy and the UN SDGs (quality education, sustainable cities, peace & strong institutions). A first-of-its-kind institution for the GCC.
