Capital raise · cultural investment

A Dubai cultural institution, made investable

Byzantine & Eastern-Christian heritage museum, Dubai — leading the investment case & capital raise (2025–present)
Challenge

Turn a landmark private collection into an investable institution — a board-ready vehicle a family office or strategic investor could underwrite, not a passion project.

What I built

The full investor package: a multi-year financial model (CAPEX, financing, monthly P&L and cash flow, 5-year IRR / NPV, scenario, sensitivity and Monte-Carlo), a ~70-page business plan, visitor and market analysis benchmarked to Louvre Abu Dhabi and the Met, and the equity and governance structure for the raise.

Positioning

Framed as cultural diplomacy — uniting Byzantine and Middle-Eastern heritage through art — aligned with the UAE’s cultural-tourism strategy and the UN SDGs (quality education, sustainable cities, peace & strong institutions). A first-of-its-kind institution for the GCC.
